## 🗂️ Projects

### 1. 🚗 BMW Used Cars — SQL Analysis
**Tools:** Microsoft SQL Server (T-SQL)

Exploratory analysis of 10,781 BMW used car listings covering pricing anomalies, long-term ownership costs, depreciation patterns, transmission trends, and fuel efficiency comparisons.

**Key techniques:** Window functions, RANK(), LAG(), subqueries, CASE statements, aggregate functions, 10-year cost modelling

**Key findings:**
- BMW i3 is the cheapest model to own long-term — lowest tax (£38/yr), least depreciation, highest efficiency
- Diesel saves £41/year in tax vs petrol and delivers 15 more miles per gallon
- 2 Series depreciates fastest (£117K range); Z3 holds value best

### 3. ☕ Coffee Sales Performance Analysis
**Tools:** Microsoft Power BI

Interactive dashboard analysing a year of transaction-level coffee shop data across time-of-day patterns, payment methods, and product performance.

**Key techniques:** Power BI slicers, DAX measures, time-of-day segmentation, revenue analysis

### 5. 🧠 Student Mental Health Risk Analysis
**Tools:** Excel + Power Query (cleaning) · Power BI + DAX (modelling & visualisation)

Cohort-based severity analysis of 101 university students — moving beyond simple prevalence counting into structured risk segmentation and intervention prioritisation.

**Key techniques:** Excel severity engineering (OR/AND logic), DAX proportional normalisation, Power Query cleaning, Power BI dashboard

**Key findings:**
- Year 3 carries the highest psychological burden at 75% prevalence — raw counts alone made Year 1 look most at-risk
- 63.4% of the cohort (64 students) report at least one mental health condition
- Only 6 of 64 affected students sought specialist care — a significant treatment gap
- Psychological burden appears across all CGPA tiers — performance-based targeting would miss most cases

🔗 [View Project Page](https://kehindeodewabi.github.io/portfolio/student-mental-health.html) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Power BI File](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1LQuy2Frco17XJB0Ex2DxSrJ0Yvf1DUZY/view?usp=drive_link) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Full Report](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1ly8WWzo55TEEib-IJWeQl0qo7nEoyKOJ/edit?usp=drive_link)
